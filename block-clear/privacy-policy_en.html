Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Block Clear is built and published by Flow Fusion Studios (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Block Clear mobile application (“the App”).

Information We Collect

  • Game progress: Scores, achievements, and session statistics stored locally on your device.
  • Device information: Anonymous technical data (device model, OS version, crash logs) to keep the game stable.
  • Game Center: If you sign in, Apple Game Center may collect data as governed by Apple’s policies.

How We Use Information

  • To save gameplay progress, leaderboards, and achievements.
  • To troubleshoot crashes and measure feature usage.
  • To deliver advertisements and reward-based video content.
  • To comply with legal requirements and platform policies.

Data Storage & Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device. Aggregated analytics and crash reports are kept for up to 24 months. Ad network data retention is managed by the respective providers.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center (leaderboards & achievements)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)

Please check each provider’s privacy notice for their data practices.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of Game Center by signing out in iOS Settings.
  • Delete the App to remove local game data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Block Clear is rated for everyone. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us for removal.
