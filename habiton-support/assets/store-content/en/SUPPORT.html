‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a habit?
Tap on a habit in your daily list to view details, then use the delete option at the bottom of the screen.
Can I edit a habit after creating it?
Yes! Tap on any habit to view its details, then tap the edit button to modify its settings.
How are streaks calculated?
Streaks count consecutive days you've completed a habit according to its frequency. If a habit isn't scheduled for a particular day, it doesn't break your streak.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data will be permanently removed.
How do I change notification settings?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings and toggle habit reminders on or off. You can also set individual reminder times when creating or editing habits.
Can I sync my data across devices?
Currently, HabitOn stores all data locally on your device for privacy. Cloud sync is not available.
How do I create a custom habit frequency?
When creating a habit, select "Custom" frequency and choose your preferred schedule (daily, weekly, or monthly intervals with specific days or dates).
Why isn't my habit showing up today?
Check your habit's frequency settings. Habits only appear on days when they're scheduled according to their frequency (daily, weekdays, custom schedule, etc.).
