Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2024

Project No Meaning ("we," "our," or "us") operates the HanGo mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Key Point: HanGo is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All your learning data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

HanGo does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Learning progress data (stored locally only)
  • Audio recordings or voice data

1.2 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your learning progress and vocabulary mastery levels
  • App settings and preferences (language selection, audio settings)
  • Daily practice streaks and statistics
  • Flashcard review history

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we cannot use it. The local data stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track your vocabulary learning progress
  • Maintain app settings and preferences
  • Enable offline functionality

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. All information remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

3.1 Third-Party Services

HanGo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Security

Your data security is ensured by design:

  • Local Storage: All data remains on your device using secure iOS/Android storage mechanisms
  • No Network Transmission: No personal data is sent over the internet
  • Device Security: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features

5. Children's Privacy

HanGo is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

6. Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Deletion: You can reset your progress or delete the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Data remains on your device and moves with app backups
  • Control: You control all settings and preferences

7. App Permissions

HanGo may request the following device permissions:

  • Audio Playback: To play Korean pronunciation audio
  • Local Storage: To save your learning progress and settings
  • Notifications (Optional): To remind you of daily practice sessions

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. International Users

HanGo complies with international privacy regulations including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) - South Korea

Since we do not collect personal data, compliance is achieved by design.

10. Open Source and Transparency

HanGo is committed to transparency. Our privacy-first approach means:

  • No hidden data collection
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No advertising networks
  • Complete local data control
