â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use the flashcards effectively?
A: Use the swipe gestures:
  • Swipe Left: "I know this word" - moves to known pile
  • Swipe Right: "I need to learn this" - keeps in learning pile
  • Swipe Up: Hide translation to test yourself
  • Swipe Down: Show translation for reference
  • Tap Speaker: Play audio pronunciation
Q: Why can't I hear the audio pronunciation?
A: Check these common solutions:
  • Ensure your device volume is turned up
  • Check that your device is not in silent mode
  • Try using headphones to test audio output
  • Restart the app if audio isn't working
  • Make sure you have the latest app version
Q: How does the spaced repetition system work?
A: Our smart learning algorithm shows you words based on how well you know them:
  • New words appear more frequently
  • Words you struggle with come back sooner
  • Well-known words appear less often for review
  • The system adapts to your individual learning pace
Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Yes! HanGo works completely offline. All vocabulary, audio, and your progress are stored locally on your device. No internet connection required for learning.
